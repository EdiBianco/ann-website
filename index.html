Ann C. Shipherd, Esq.

Licensed Customs Broker & International Trade Attorney

Ann C. Shipherd is an International Trade and Customs attorney with over 15 years of extensive industry experience in imports, exports, customs, transportation, and trade compliance. A licensed United States Customs Broker with a Juris Doctor from Stetson University College of Law, Shipherd possesses the practical expertise to zealously represent her clients in achieving exceptional results while navigating the complexities of international commerce.

She has counseled various industry stakeholders, multinational corporations, importers, exporters, manufacturers, freight forwarders, and global financial institutions through an array of legal matters, including customs and trade compliance, data privacy, import and export compliance, transportation and supply chain, commercial banking, and regulatory concerns.
